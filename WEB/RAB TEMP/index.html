About

A

Hi, I am Sam Kebbell. A freelance web designer, front-end developer and digital planner based in UK.

"I'm a dedicated sports enthusiast, spending my weekends immersed in the excitement of football, cricket, and rugby, with a lifelong passion for Norwich City FC, where I cheer for the Canaries through every match. My love for sports is matched by my deep appreciation for music, which serves as my universal language. I enjoy all genres, from jazz and classical to hip-hop and electronic, and I'm an avid crate digger, constantly on the hunt for rare vinyl records and hidden gems. Additionally, Japan's rich culture and cuisine captivate me, with the country's unique blend of tradition and modernity—from the vibrant streets of Tokyo to the serene temples of Kyoto—holding a special place in my heart."
