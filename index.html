About

A

Hello, I’m Sam Kebbell, a dedicated web designer, front-end developer, and digital planner based in the UK.

I specialize in building visually captivating and highly interactive websites using my advanced knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. With a deep expertise in Visual Studio Code, Git, and GitHub, I ensure efficient and streamlined workflows for every project. I’m also skilled in React and Ruby, with intermediate proficiency, and have experience using Adobe Creative Suite and Microsoft Office for a range of design and project management tasks. Additionally, I have a foundational understanding of Bootstrap, Figma, and SASS, allowing me to seamlessly adapt to different project needs and collaborate effectively across diverse teams.

Beyond the screen, I’m a passionate sports enthusiast, dedicating my weekends to the excitement of football, cricket, and rugby, and cheering on Norwich City FC as a lifelong fan of the Canaries. My love for sports is complemented by my deep appreciation for music, which I see as a universal language. Whether it’s jazz, classical, hip-hop, or electronic, I’m an avid crate digger, always searching for rare vinyl records and hidden gems. I’m also captivated by Japan's unique blend of tradition and modernity, from the vibrant streets of Tokyo to the peaceful temples of Kyoto, finding endless inspiration in its culture and cuisine. Let’s collaborate to transform your ideas into stunning digital experiences with innovative design and robust development. Together, we can create something truly exceptional.
